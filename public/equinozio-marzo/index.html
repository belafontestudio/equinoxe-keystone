Equinoxe, for its 30th Anniversary, wants to celebrate the love for travels and for discovering new places. We and our crews have visited countries all over the world and selected for you some destinations we particularly loved during our trips.
Every place is paired with one of our charter crewed yachts: from the ones with a more adventurous spirit to the more relaxing ones, we tried to capture the essence of the destination and to combine it with the character of our yachts. Which type of traveller are you? Let’s find out.

A peaceful Maldivian break on Dhoni Stella

A traditional yacht for people who are looking for the calm of a family holiday. “Maldives” means relax, but also great outdoor experiences. Swimming, surfing, diving: the opportunities for dynamic travellers who want to explore the local natural beauties are endless.

Contact us

“not being bound to an island and having access to multiple destinations while in complete comfort really made our holiday far beyond what a resort stay would have been.” Dave and Tanya

Explore the Maldives on Dhoni Stella, a luxury yacht designed for couples and families. With the two large suites on board and the sundeck, you will have your personal, floating resort. A classic destination for unusual travellers: you will design your cruise in the Maldives, choosing the islands to visit and the activities for your freetime.
The islands are full of surprises, from the markets and mosques in Malé, to the surfing spots on Meemu Atoll, from the coral reefs and the sealife wonders of the Huvadhu Atholhu, to the crystal clear waters of Villingili. The Maldives, a unique vacation spot for who is looking for relax, but also for who likes to try new sports and activities.

Sail to Sardinia with style on Dione Star

Dione Star brings you to Sardinia, for an unforgettable holiday rich of great views and relaxing moments on one of the small beaches in the southern part of the island. A classy experience on our most refined yacht.

Contact us

“Our family vacation on the Dione Star was perfect: the combination of style and charm was exactly what we were looking for.” Mark and Michelle

Dione Star is perfect for a relaxing holiday: its design interiors are crafted for giving you maximum comfort with a special attention to style and elegance. Every detail is important, from the tableware to the excellent mahogany fittings in the cabines. Even equipped with engine, the yacht remains environmentally-friendly: antipollution and wastewater treatments conform the Dione Star to international standards.
Sardinia is one of the most beautiful paradise in the Mediterranean Sea. Admire the annual exhibition of sculptures in Teulada, lie down on the solitary beaches of Sant’Antioco and lose yourself in the small streets of Carloforte, the only village on San Pietro Island. Turquoise sea and white beaches will make you forget every other exhotic destination you’ve always dreamt about.

Dharma, an adventurous sailing discovering the Dalmatian coast

If you think that there is no holiday without a bit of adventure, then Dharma is the right yacht for you. Bring your friends and sail to the beautiful sea of Croatia, where the wilderness of the landscape meets Roman history.

Contact us

“On Dharma you can follow your passion for sailing and kitesurf. At the end of every trip, my guest always wish they could never leave.” Matteo, the captain

Dharma can host up to 8 guests in its four double cabines. The on-board facilities are meant to please every sportsperson: kite surf, snorkeling, water sky, wake board and fishing equipment give a wide choice when it comes down to entertainment. Matteo, the captain, knows every secret of the Dalmatian coast and his experience will be a precious guide during your journey.
Discover the two thousands-year old history of Pula, then sail to the bay of Šibenik and to the lush landscape of its 220 islands. Taste the local products at the market in Split, the city chosed by Emperor Diocletian for his retirement. Admire the beauty of Hvar, one of the world’s finest islands and discover Dubrovnik, the enchanting “Pearl of the Adriatic”.

Feel So Good: an amazing outdoor experience in the Seychelles

If you love marine landscapes and outdoor activities, escape from the grey sky of the city and sail to the Seichelles archipelago, a peaceful natural paradise. Feel So Good and its crew will accompany you in your journey through one of the most exotic places in the world.

Contact us

“Stefano is an excellent captain, with an amusing Italian sense of humor. The crew is simply unforgettable, especially Barbara and her delicious cakes. The Seichelles are faboulous, a place where you “Feel So Good”!” James and Martha

The Feel So Good is the ideal match for groups up to 8 people who want to stay in contact with nature during their holiday. Have fun in the turquoise waters of the Seichelles, trying every kind of water sport with the yacht’s equipment, from kayak to snorkeling.
The Seichelles are renowed for their clear sea and white beaches, but they can defintely offer more than that. Go beyond the beach and hike in a lush jungle, or swim underwater to see the coral reefs and the unique sealife. Your dream of escaping the daily routine for a few weeks is only a small click away.

Whitefin, an exciting race in the waters of Tuscany

If what you love most about yachts is speed, then you’ll love the Whitefin, designed by Bruce King, famous for his ocean racing and maxi yachts. Feel the warm wind of Elba Island and discover the wild nature of the Tuscan archipelago.

Contact us

“ I really enjoyed my time on-board. Whether participating or relaxing mid-deck, being on-board the newly refurbished Whitefin is a truly amazing experience. I couldn’t imagine a better way to see and experience the Mediterranean.” Stephen

The Whitefin, with its agility and lightness, is a true champion. Winner of Trofeo Prada and Maxi Yacht Rolex Cup, it will surprise you with its speed and advanced technological equipment. A special care has also been dedicated to the interior design, that recalls to mind the timeless elegance of 19th century, with the fireplace and piano in the saloon, the VIP guest cabin and the warm wooden furniture.
Discover one of Italy’s most beautiful islands, Elba. Famous for its beaches, every one different from the other, Elba is also the right place for sport-lovers, from snorkeling to golf and even hiking. The island gives you also the opportunity to immerse into the Italian ancient and modern history, with the Etruscan and Roman ruins and the residences from the Napoleonic age.

Myra, your passage to the Tyrrhenian Sea

Sail to the Pontine Islands and the Gulf of Naples on the spacious Myra, and discover the natural beauties and the traditions of the Italian islands. From the sandy beaches and natural pools of Ponza to the grottos and glamorous nightlife of Capri, this yacht is perfect for who loves to travel in good company, without renouncing to comforts.

Contact us

“Sailing with Myra was a fantastic experience. Everything has been wonderful, from the capable caring crew and amazing chef, to catching our own sushi.” Jim and Deborah

The sailing schooner Myra is the ideal choice for groups between 6 and 12 people, with its 6 cabins and spacious interiors. The yacht is provided with every comfort, from flat screen TV to sun pads on the deck. Its crew has a long experience in the navigation of the Tyrrhenian Sea and in the local wine and food traditions. Massimo, the captain, is a wine expert and will guide you in the discover of the local flavours together with the chef, who will introduce you to the local specialties. A true journey in the Italian culture.
The itinerary will bring you to the Pontine Islands, characterized by the contrast between sandy and rocky beaches, clear sea, and high cliffs. Rich of ancient history, the islands were already used by Romans as vacation spot. The journey will continue towards the Gulf of Naples, where you will admire the Blue Grotto near Capri; and the gardens and the Aragonese Castle in Ischia.
