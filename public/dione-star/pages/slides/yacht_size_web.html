— The Yacht —

Built in 1991 by a reputable Dutch yard, Dione Star is a classic example of fundamental refinement. She was extensively modernised between 2011-2013 and now boasts every creature comfort you could wish for. Whether your dream is to island hop in the Caribbean, or make a trans-Atlantic passage, Dione Star will carry you in supreme comfort and safety.

The new owner spared no expense during the two-year refit, and worked closely with the original designers to make the yacht even better than new. With the Lloyds-class steel hull lengthened to 43.8m, there is ample accommodation for up to 12 guests, as well as the professional crew of 11.

Bimini

The aft, mostly covered by a hard top Bimini, is the best place to sit under navigation, shaded, and protected from wind and spray. Two coffee tables in the center of the “U” shaped sofa raise and double up for comfortable dining. Ahead of the sofa, while at anchor, you might have dinner in a more conventional array of rectangular tables and director chairs. A couple of hamacs hang around the shaded area in good weather.

Fly

On the top deck, ahead of the wheelhouse, two separate areas can hosts independent parties with relative independence.  An enclosed open-air living room area, and a sun bathing cushioned platform.
The open-air living room, built around a protected sofa with two low square coffee tables, transforms into a dining area when the tables enlarge to form, if desirable, a large rectangular 1,70 on 3,40 mtrs table, sitting 18. The whole area can also be covered with mattresses, and turns into a truly comfortable reading and sunbathing zone while sailing. At anchor, an awning often shades it.
Still on the Fly deck, Ahead of the enclosed sofa, a large open deck of m 3,80 on 5,80, completely covered with mattresses, and side back rests, is a favorite for lying down while swimming from the boat. Here, a table can raise up from the teakwood flooring, for dining when docked in in warm weather.

The Stern

The stern of Dione opens up to form a small swimming and landing platform. It has two separate swimming ladders with different inclinations, and director chairs for intimate chats. From here guests enjoy fishing in the evenings, while under way, fishing is best from the flat bridge behind the Bimini sofa.
