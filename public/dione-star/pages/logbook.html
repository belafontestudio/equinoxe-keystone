Dione Star’s owner is passionate about the sea, and about providing a remarkable experience for his guests. During the yacht’s two-year refit, he was keen to not only lengthen the hull and upgrade most specifications, but also to install highly dependable systems to allow the yacht to roam the world without constraint.

safety and simplicity. The intensive re-modelling was undertaken by specialist refit yards between 2011 and 2013. Key to the transformation were two leading design teams, Diana Yachts, who originally conceived the vessel, and Nauta Yachts of Milan, Italy, who oversaw the redesign of the bridge, decks, and part of the interiors.

Win-win solution

The result is a yacht with a greater range, more dependable systems, surplus power and a larger internal volume. The owner’s emphasis on the new accommodation and social space has delivered an elegant and yet homely ambience in which families and friends can fully relax.

Highest standards

From the outset, the project scrupulously followed the exhaustive criteria for Lloyd's Charter classification. The steel hull is now rated to the A100 Maltese Cross certificate, the highest possible standard obtainable, whilst the wastewater treatment, antifouling system, and engine emissions all comply with the strict Cayman Charter legislation. Coincidentally, these also meet the IMO’s (International Maritime Organisation) tough MARPOL convention for preventing pollution from ships.

  • Highest standards
  • Comfort

Comfort

Smooth running engines, quiet generators and purified water systems all help guests to enjoy their cruising grounds to the full. As such, the wastewater is fully treated before being safely discharged, and the fuel is also purified using Racor filters and an Alfa Laval centrifuge system to prevent stoppages. There are separators and collectors for bilge water and waste oil, to prevent even the slightest drop of pollution entering the clear turquoise waters of the Med, the Caribbean or the Pacific.

Home from home

With up to 80kW of power available, and 28,800 liters of desalinated water on tap every day, Dione Star is able to treat it’s guests with all the comforts they would find ashore, and a few more besides. There is a laundry with three washing machines and three driers, so you won’t need to carry a huge amount of luggage. The cold store has no less than four refrigerators, two of 220 liters, and one 70 liters, plus three freezers of 200 liters, so the chef can store any number of delicacies, or preserve your own catch. The tenders can also race ashore to pick up fresh local delicacies for an evening’s fine dining.

Caribbean

There is no better way to explore over 1,500 miles of sandy beaches, azure water and hidden bays along the Caribbean route than by yacht. Dione Star operates an itinerary that stretches from the shallow, languid waters of the Bahamas right down to the vast seascapes Venezuela, with the Captain and crew knowing a few special places well off the beaten track. Whether you want to dive stunning coral reefs, laze under the tropical sun, or visit vibrant and dynamic ports, your ideal Caribbean cruise can be planned to perfection.

Ocean Crossing

As Dione Star is moved between her seasonal venues, there is a great opportunity to enjoy a very special deep-sea experience, namely passage-making under sail. Starting from either the USA or Europe, you can join this purposeful yacht for a fortnight of trans-Atlantic sailing, harnessing the power of the wind to cover more than 200 miles a day. Watch dolphins cavorting under the bow, feel the easy motion as the yacht heels to the breeze, and spend unhurried time with your family and friends. If you want some practical experience, you can take a turn at the wheel, learn about the navigation systems, or trim the sails, all under the friendly guidance of the Captain. After all, the term ‘sea change’ wasn’t invented by accident. As the yacht travels gently between the time zones with no risk of jet lag, you will find yourself relaxing to the rhythm of the ocean.

Mediterranean

The whole of the Mediterranean Sea is yours to explore in the most comfortable way, and with total flexibility. From the lively Spanish Coast to the remotest islands of Greece and Turkey, Dione Star will transport you between borders to enjoy the very best the Med has to offer. The crew will take you into idyllic anchorages and the most exclusive marinas in areas such as the Cote d’Azur, the Aeolian Islands or the Amalfi coast. You can enjoy your favourite cruising grounds in complete comfort, and perhaps discover a few you did not even know existed along the way.
